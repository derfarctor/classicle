How To Play

Welcome to Classicle's Word Polygon. From the letters shown in the polygon above, try and make as many Latin words of or more letters, making sure to use the middle letter of the polygon in every word. Answers must not be plurals (nouns will be singular nominative), conjugated verbs (only principle parts are accepted), comparatives or superlatives.
