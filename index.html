How To Play

Welcome to Classicle's daily Word Polygon. From the letters shown in the polygon above, try and make as many Latin words of or more letters, making sure to use the centre letter of the polygon in every word. For each word, a letter can only be used as many times as it appears in the polygon.

Answers must not be plurals (only singular nominative is accepted), conjugated verbs (only principal parts are accepted), comparatives or superlatives. Classicle does not use the letter j - always use an i instead.

Tip: Hover over or tap any Latin word to see a translation!
